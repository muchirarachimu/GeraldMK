Benefits of ICT Systems in Business

New Products, Services and Business Models

IT systems and are major enabling tools for firms to create new products or services and business models.

Customer and Supplier Intimacy

Serving customers well leads to their satisfaction which then leads to customers returning, thus raising revenues and profits.

Improved Decision Making

Information systems have made it possible for managers to use real time data from the market place when making crucial decisions.

Competitive Advantage

IT enables business to provide a product/service to customers better than competitors e.g. responding to customers and suppliers in real time.
